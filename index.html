Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Verba.

Is Verba free to use?
The browser demo on this page is completely free with no account needed. Our full MVP is also free to try right now. When we launch the full product we're planning $6 a month with a free tier so you can get started without paying anything.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. The browser demo works on Android in Chrome and on iPhone in Safari or Chrome. For the best experience with the live demo we recommend Chrome on desktop or Android. The full MVP linked above works on any mobile device.
How is this different from just recording myself?
Recording yourself means watching a video after the fact to catch your mistakes. Verba catches them the moment they happen: your phone vibrates and the screen flashes the instant you say um, uh, or like. That real-time feedback is what actually breaks the habit.
Does Verba store my voice or speech data?
The browser demo processes everything locally in your browser, so nothing is sent to any server. Your speech never leaves your device when using the on-page demo.
What languages does Verba support?
The browser demo currently supports English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, German, Japanese, Arabic, Hindi, Korean, Italian, and Russian. Select your language from the selector before starting your session and Verba will transcribe and detect filler words in that language.
When will the full app launch?
We don't have a fixed date yet. We're focused on building it right rather than building it fast. Join the waitlist above and you'll be the first to know.
Can I track my own specific filler words?
Yes. In the live demo there is a custom word tracker field where you can type any word you want Verba to detect. If you say “basically” or “honestly” or any other word constantly, type it in before starting your session and Verba will track it alongside um, uh, and like.
